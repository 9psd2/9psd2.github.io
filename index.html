The Musician

Peyt Spencer Dewar, also known as PSD, is an emcee and University of Florida alumnus with a B.S. in Computer Science Engineering. He is a versatile artist who can also produce his own beats, understanding the fluid and dynamic mechanics of sound that allows him to create unique instrumentals. With thoughtful insight into the fundamentals of hip-hop and a flow that wastes no words, lyrics, or bars, PSD strings together soliloquies comprised of meaningful metaphors, playful paradoxes, and philosophical puns. These allow his audience to actively engage with his music while enjoying the plethora of good-humored lyrics. Past performances include Gatormania and the Alachua County Fair.
